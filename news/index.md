# Changelog

## complexNet 0.2.0

CRAN release: 2022-11-10

### Major changes

- Adding two new functions (make_kp and iterate_kp) that allow to
  generate and iterate kp networks, where k is the degree a new
  individual is aiming for and p is the proportion of socially inherited
  links (1-p is the proportion of random links).
